Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. Our game uses Google Firebase services to improve the gaming experience.

Currently, we use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous gameplay data, such as levels played and game launch frequency.

In the future, we may use other Firebase services such as:

  • Crashlytics – to track and fix unexpected crashes
  • Remote Config – to update game parameters without releasing a new version
  • Cloud Messaging – to send optional in-game notifications

No personally identifiable information (such as name, email, or phone number) is collected, stored, or shared.

If you have any questions or would like to opt out of analytics tracking, please contact us at gzhhong@gmail.com.
