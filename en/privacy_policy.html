Privacy Policy – AimBang

Last updated: October 2025

Welcome to AimBang, a mobile shooting game developed with Unity. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

AimBang does not collect any personally identifiable information. The game uses Unity Analytics, a service provided by Unity Technologies, to collect anonymous usage data such as gameplay duration, crash reports, and device performance information.

2. How We Use Data

The data collected is used only to improve the gameplay experience, identify bugs, and enhance performance. We do not sell, rent, or share data for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

AimBang uses Unity Analytics. For more information, please review Unity’s Privacy Policy.

4. Children’s Privacy

This game is suitable for general audiences and does not target children under 13.

5. Data Retention

Anonymous data may be retained by Unity Analytics for statistical analysis and cannot identify individual users.
