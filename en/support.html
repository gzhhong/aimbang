FAQs

  • Can't find the aiming point in red?
    • Please move your other hand out of the view of the camera and try again.
  • The aiming point in red is shaking badly
    • The finger should be perpendicular to the screen
  • Need more firepower?
    • Try aiming and firing at the target using both your index and middle fingers simultaneously.
